I am a PhD candidate at CREST centre, in the Department of Computer Science, at University College London.
As a member of the SOLAR team, under the supervision of Professor Federica Sarro and Professor Mark Harman, I am working on Predictive Models for Software Analysis and Search-Based Software Engineering with a focus on Software Effort Estimation and Agile Methods.

I did my Masters' in Software Engineering at Tarbiat Modares University, and worked as a Software Developer in industry for more than 7 years. [Expanded CV ▼ ]
